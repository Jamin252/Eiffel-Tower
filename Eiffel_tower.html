La Tour Eiffel

Paris, France

Constructed 1887-1889

Eiffel Tower

The Construction Schedule

  1. The first floor was complete on the 1st April 1888
  2. The second floor was complete on the 14th August 1888
  3. The top section was complete on the 31st March 1889

Facts and figures

  • The tower has 1665 steps
  • 2.5 million rivets were used
  • 7,300 tonnes of iron
  • 60 tonnes of paint
